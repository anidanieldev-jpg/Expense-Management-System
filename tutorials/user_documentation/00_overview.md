## Glossary

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Vendor** | A supplier or service provider you owe money to |
| **Expense** | A specific bill or invoice from a vendor |
| **Wallet** | An account you pay from (Cash, Bank, Mobile Money) |
| **Payment** | Moving money OUT to pay off expenses |
| **Deposit** | Money coming IN (vendor transfers, refunds) |
| **Push** | Upload local changes to Google Sheets |
